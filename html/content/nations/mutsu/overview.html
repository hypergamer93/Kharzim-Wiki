The Kingdom of Mutsu was established in 385 SS by Mutsu I, to unite the northern coast of Westland Kharzim. Eventually, they started expanding south, until they hit water. Later on, after they started interacting with the Sershan Empire, they discovered a decently sized island off the west coast, and so they started settling there in around 427 SS. In 454 SS, King Mutsu III entrusted his closest Duke, Gortreoza to govern his own land, and formed the Duchy of Gortreoza.

Currently ruled by King Mutsu IX
